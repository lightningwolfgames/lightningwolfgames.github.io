WHAT IS Animecha
Animecha is a roguelite twin-stick shooter-deflector. Select your Animecha, defeat waves of enemies, discover new weapons and combinations, upgrade your arsenal and use powerful defensive abilities to last against increasingly tougher enemies and bosses.

Twin-stick Shooter-Deflector

Unlike traditional twin-stick shooters, Animecha uses the second stick to aim deflector shields to hold back enemy waves, reflect damage and unleash powerful defensive moves to keep you alive. Then use your weapon systems to take out enemies coming at you from other angles. Manage which lanes you deflect and which lanes you attack to survive.

Roguelite

In Animecha you start each run with a basic mech and gain XP as you kill enemies. XP can be spent to equip new weapons, defensive skills and upgrades to help you progress further and deal more damage. Between runs you can use currency to upgrade the base stats of your mechs to help you in future runs.
